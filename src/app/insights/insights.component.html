Key Insights and Intelligence summary

  • Number of Products from DarkWeb has been doubled from 2016 to 2017
  • Number of Sellers have exponentially increased 300% by 2017 from 690 to 2090
  • The phishing activities are on the consistent rise targeting Payment Services reaching approx. 15,000 attacks until Oct 2020
  • Number of attacks on the payment services domain are on the rise and most prone to the attack, followed by Banks and Govt Organizations
  • Keywords such as premium lifetime, CVV are more prominently used by underground sellers

Intelligence Dissemination

Due to the different stakeholders that will be using the intelligence gathered, the dissemination thereof will be tailored for different audience and scenarios

Within the financial sector
  • Tailored reports will be sent out to the industry90 to 2090
  • Partners within the sector will get access to automated feeds and APIs for to make sure that they always have access to the latest intelligence
  • A searchable knowledge base will be made available
Within the company
  • Quarterly AI4Cyber awareness sessions to sensitize all employees and customers
  • Monthly tailored reports targeting employees and customers at different levels
  • Where a user is directly targeted, automated alerts will be sent out
  • All users will have access to automated feeds and APIs for real-time protection

Courses of action based on intelligence

  • Make sure no malicious e-mail reach users: Spam filter on the mail server to scan all incoming mails, flag and block those that fail to meet security criteria
  • Block all unauthorized access attempts: Firewalls should include the gathered intelligence in all security scans
  • Minimize the possible points of attack: All ports that are not being used on the firewall to be blocked and those in production to be constantly monitored
  • Detect all breach attempts: An intrusion prevention system to be installed to improve security and get more value from the gathered intelligence
  • Improve client devices security: Implement two factor authentication logins
  • After all courses of action have been implemented, vulnerability assessments should be done frequently for continuous improvement
  • The above is suitable for our organization because the solutions address our immediate cyber problems while leaving room for future upgrades
